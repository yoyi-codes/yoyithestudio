Hi, I’m Johana, known as YOYI.

HI, I'M YOYI!

Digital Artist & Multimedia Designer

I'm a digital artist and multimedia designer, born in Caracas, Venezuela, and currently based in Buenos Aires. I've been working on creative projects for over 6 years while finishing my degree in Multimedia Design at Escuela Da Vinci .

During this time, I’ve worked on illustration, visual identity, social media content, streaming platforms, music releases, animation, and video editing. I love jumping between disciplines because every project lets me learn something new and find different ways to communicate an idea.

(Or maybe I'm just a nerd with way too many interests) ✦
"EVEN THOUGH MY WORK IS FULL OF COLOR, PLAY, AND EXPERIMENTATION, I TAKE EVERYTHING I DO VERY SERIOUSLY."

I'm a highly curious person. I'm drawn to art, music, design, fashion, illustration, books, and storytelling in all its forms. I truly enjoy researching, observing, and connecting references.

I like to understand the why behind every decision and tackle new challenges. If I don't know how to do something today, I'll probably be researching it by tomorrow.

I love collaborating with people who have ambitious projects, different perspectives, and a drive to build something authentic .

PS: If you made it this far, thanks for stopping by my site and taking the time to see what I do, it means more than you'd imagine!
